Knut Olaf Kals is a Norwegian painter. In his artistic carear he has buildt what has grown to become a considerable collection. He finds his motivation in love for humanity and nature.

This website was created in Knut Olaf's name by his nephew Morten Kals, intended to help promote his artistry by sharing some select projects.

Frå Sundjord til Rio



Kranselag, Hersleb

As the renovation of Hersleb School is approaching its conclusion, the entire workforce was invited to tipping out (kranselag). Knut Olaf’s pictures featured prominently on stage, accompanying the cultural experience of live piano brilliance from one of our renowned masters, Wolfgang Plagge.
