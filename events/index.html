Knut Olaf Kals is a Norwegian painter. In his artistic carear he has buildt what has grown to become a considerable collection. He finds his motivation in love for humanity and nature.

This website was created in Knut Olaf's name by his nephew Morten Kals, intended to help promote his artistry by sharing some select projects.
